When We Started Dating

After a year and a half of working together, we discovered that maybe we liked each other as more than co-workers! It didn't take long for a strong relationship to form outside of work and before we knew it, we were inseperable and committed!

The Proposal

On our one year anniversary, Shane made reservations for a hotel and dinner at a fancy restaurant. Carly was not at all fooled, but she let him think she didn't know what was up. After dinner, we took a late walk around the forks reminiscing about the times we had spent there early in our relationship. After some nervous fumbling, Shane got down on one knee and asked Carly to marry him. Though maybe she knew it was coming, it was still the happiest moment of her life!
